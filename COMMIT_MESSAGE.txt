Add biblical angel names to glossary and update Google Cloud PhraseSet

This commit adds seven important biblical angel names to the glossary.json
file to improve speech recognition accuracy for these theological terms
during transcription. The changes have been synchronized with the Google
Cloud Speech-to-Text PhraseSet to ensure immediate availability for
production use.

## Changes Made

### Glossary Updates (glossary.json)
Added the following angel names in alphabetical order to maintain consistency
with the existing glossary structure:

1. **Abaddon** (line 205)
   - Added after "Absalom's Monument"
   - Hebrew name meaning "destruction" or "place of destruction"
   - Referenced in Revelation 9:11 as the angel of the bottomless pit

2. **Apollyon** (line 244)
   - Added after "Antipatris"
   - Greek name meaning "The Destroyer"
   - Referenced in Revelation 9:11 as the Greek equivalent of Abaddon

3. **Gabriel** (line 439)
   - Added before "Gabatha"
   - Archangel known as a messenger angel
   - Delivered announcements of John the Baptist's and Jesus Christ's births
   - Appears in Daniel and Luke

4. **Jeremiel** (line 4795)
   - Added after "Jeremoth"
   - Also known as Jerahmeel
   - Mentioned in 2 Esdras as an archangel in certain traditions

5. **Michael** (line 5611)
   - Added after "Michaiah"
   - Archangel and warrior angel
   - Protector of Israel, leads heavenly armies against evil
   - Referenced in Daniel, Jude, and Revelation

6. **Raphael** (line 6292)
   - Added after "Rapha"
   - Archangel associated with healing and guidance
   - Appears in the Book of Tobit, assisting Tobias during his journey

7. **Sariel** (line 6427)
   - Added after "Sacar"
   - Identified in the Book of Enoch as one of the seven holy angels
   - Watches over humanity

### Existing Angel Names Verified
The following angel names were already present in the glossary:
- Jerahmeel (line 4780)
- Phanuel (line 6115) - Fourth angel who stands before God (Book of Enoch)
- Raguel (line 6265) - Archangel of justice, fairness, harmony, vengeance, and redemption
- Uriel (line 7264) - Mentioned in 2 Esdras, recognized as an archangel

### Data Quality Improvements
- Removed duplicate "Abaddon" entry that was incorrectly placed after "Zurishaddai"
- All entries maintain proper alphabetical ordering
- Total phrase count increased from 6,614 to 6,620 phrases

## Google Cloud PhraseSet Synchronization

Successfully updated the Google Cloud Speech-to-Text PhraseSet:
- **PhraseSet Name**: church-glossary-10k
- **Project ID**: 222662040787
- **Location**: global
- **Full Resource Path**: projects/222662040787/locations/global/phraseSets/church-glossary-10k
